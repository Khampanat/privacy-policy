Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

English

PTCalc Coins values your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information:

  • Data Collection: We collect your email address via Google Sign-In for authentication purposes only.
  • Data Usage: Your data is used solely to provide app functionality and secure your account access.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, or trade your personal data with any third parties.
  • Data Storage: Your investment data is stored locally on your device or within your private Firebase account.
